Title: ISU Dining Meal Plans and Rates
Source Type: Official ISU Dining page
Source URL: https://www.dining.iastate.edu/meal-plans/
Date Collected: June 9, 2026

Content:
The ISU Dining meal plan page explains the official meal plan options available to students. Meal plans are built around dining center meals, express meals, and Dining Dollars. Different plans offer different combinations of meals and flexible spending.

The meal plan page lists plans such as Cardinal, Gold, and Campanile. These plans vary by how many dining center meals are included per semester, how many express meals can be used per day, how many Dining Dollars are included, and whether guest meals are included. Dining Dollars can be used at campus dining locations, cafes, convenience stores, and other ISU Dining retail locations.

The official page is useful because it explains how the meal plans work, but it does not fully answer whether students feel the plans are worth the cost. For that, student discussion sources are useful because students often talk about whether they used all their swipes, whether they had leftover Dining Dollars, or whether a cheaper plan would have been enough.

Useful facts from this source:

ISU meal plans include dining center meals, express meals, and Dining Dollars.
Some plans include unlimited or large numbers of dining center meals.
Dining Dollars provide flexible spending at ISU Dining locations.
Official meal plan pages explain the structure, but not student satisfaction.
