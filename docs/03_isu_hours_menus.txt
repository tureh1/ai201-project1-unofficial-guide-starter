Title: ISU Dining Hours and Menus
Source Type: Official ISU Dining page
Source URL: https://www.dining.iastate.edu/hours-menus/
Date Collected: June 9, 2026

Content:
The ISU Dining Hours and Menus page provides official information about when dining locations are open and what food is available. Students can use this page to check current dining center hours, cafe hours, market hours, menus, and payment types.

Official ISU Dining location results include Friley Windows, Richardson Court Marketplace, and Union Drive Marketplace. These are major dining center options that students may compare when deciding where to eat. Other official dining location results list cafes such as Bookends Cafe, Business Cafe, Charging Station, Courtyard Cafe, Design Cafe, and Gentle Doctor Cafe.

The official location pages can also show whether a location accepts payment methods such as credit cards, Dining Dollars, Flex Meals, meal plans, or an ISU Card. For example, search results for Friley Windows show that it accepts credit, Dining Dollars, Flex Meal, Meal Plan, and ISU Card.

The official hours and menus page is useful because hours change by day, week, semester, and break periods. Students should use the official page for current hours instead of relying on old Reddit comments.

This source is best for factual questions such as where to check hours, what locations exist, what payment types are accepted, and where to find menus. It is not the best source for questions about which food tastes best or which dining hall students prefer.
