Title: ISU Dining GET & Go
Source Type: Official ISU Dining page
Source URL: https://www.dining.iastate.edu/get-go/
Date Collected: June 9, 2026

Content:
GET & Go is an ISU Dining option for students who need food quickly. The official GET & Go page describes it as a solution for busy schedules. Students can use a meal swipe through the GET Mobile app to get food on the go.

The page explains that GET & Go is available at multiple locations. It is meant for convenience when students do not have time to sit down in a dining center. The page also tells students to review each location’s menu items and check-in details before using GET & Go.

GET & Go is important for student-life questions because many students need fast meals between classes, work, or activities. It may be especially useful for students with meal plans who want to use a swipe without spending time at a full dining hall.
