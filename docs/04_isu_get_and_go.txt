Title: ISU Dining GET & Go / Express Meals
Source Type: Official ISU Dining page
Source URL: https://www.dining.iastate.edu/get-go/
Date Collected: June 9, 2026

Content:
GET & Go, also described through ISU Dining Express options, is a quick meal option for students who do not have time to sit down in a dining center. It is designed for busy students who need food between classes, work, or other activities.

The Iowa State catalog explains that GET & Go lets students use one dining center swipe to grab a quick meal at one of five GET & Go locations. Students order through the GET app and can use dining center meals at these locations up to two times a day.

Official search results for ISU Dining Express say students can use a meal swipe through a mobile ordering app to get food from Express locations. Search results also mention five Express locations and a limit of two Express or GET & Go meals per day.

GET & Go is useful for students with meal plans because it gives them a faster way to use a swipe without eating inside a full dining hall. It is especially relevant for students with tight schedules, short breaks between classes, or days when they need food to go.
